ABOUT ASANTE

We want to save many through creativity, innovation and positivity!

We are looking to develop an online rewards-based system that will identify landlords in sub-Saharan Africa willing to charge their tenants discounted or no rental fees in this harsh economic situation brought about by Covid-19. .

We look to develop a large online community of different stakeholders looking to reward these important players that will raise the burden of rental fees off more than 360 million underprivileged people.

OUR PROCESS

1

REGISTER TENANT INFORMATION

Asante collects and validate tenant requests for rent fee waivers through an online form and as well obtains details about their respective landlords.

2

TENANT INFORMATION ANALYSIS AND VALIDATION

Asante will use an algorithm to sort and determine tenants in need of rent fee waivers based on income earned and will develop different discount offers to propose to the respective landlord

3

LANDLORD OUTREACH

Asante will reach out through social media and other online channels to landlords with proposals from data obtained from their tenants on proposed rent fee discounts

4

LANDLORD REWARD DISBURSEMENT

Asante will disburse rewards to these landlords including Certificates of Appreciations and plaques with major international organizations as signatories. There will be an automatic entry to a raffle draw that will have cash prizes and discount offers .

REWARDS TO LANDLORDS

1

CERTIFICATES OF APPRECIATION

Asante will provide certificates of appreciation and plaques endorsed by major international organisations like the World Health Organization(WHO) and other government entities leading the fight against COVID-19.

2

DISCOUNTED OFFERS ON INTERNET SERVICES AND E-COMMERCE WEBSITES

Asante is looking to develop strong partnerships with major companies in Uganda already spearheading the fight against COVID-19. From this, we will be able to provide these offers to our network of Landlords

3

GENERAL RAFFLE DRAW

All Landlords in Asante's system will automatically enter a raffle draw to win cash prizes and other gifts obtained by Asante through crowd funding
