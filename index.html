WE SAY ASANTE

We are a group of online volunteers looking to create an online rewards-based system that will identify and convince landlords in sub-Saharan Africa to charge their tenants discounted or no rental accommodation fees. These tenants are medical workers, casual labourers and many working class people who due to the coronavirus outbreak, will not be able to afford to pay rent among other needs.

COVID-19 has affected so many globally. Millions are not going to be able to afford rent for this month because of lockdown.


Finding it hard to pay your rent this month? Willing to charge your tenants discounted or no rental fees this month?
